  • Essam Debie, Kamran Shafi, Kathryn Merrick, Chris Lokan, “Reduct based ensemble of learning classifier system for real-valued classification problems,” in Computational Intelligence and Ensemble Learning (CIEL), IEEE Symposium on. IEEE, 2013.
