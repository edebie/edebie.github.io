Research Focus

My research programme develops AI-driven systems that bridge computational intelligence with real-world human and robotic operations, anchored in the cyber-physical domain.

Human-Autonomy Interaction

Adaptive AI that modulates assistance to human operators based on real-time cognitive load, measured through EEG and multi-modal physiological signals.

Swarm Intelligence & Multi-Robot Systems

Shepherding, coordination, and path planning for UAV/UGV swarms, including human-swarm teaming transparency and trust architectures.

Explainable & Transparent AI

Interpretable decision-making frameworks for autonomous systems, enabling meaningful human oversight in high-stakes operations.

AI for Cybersecurity

Privacy-preserving machine learning (GANs, federated learning), EEG-based biometric authentication, and intrusion detection for IoT environments.
